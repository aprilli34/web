    The anime Attack on Titan focuses on Eren Yeager.

    He was only a young boy when the walls protecting the area he is in was broken. Titans poured in the wall and killed all the citizens. He vowed to kill all the titans when his hometown was completely wrecked and his mom eaten. He was forced to flee into the next wall inside with his friends Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlet. When Eren finally came of age, he and his friends started training. Mikasa excelled in training while Eren seemed to be failing. Still, Eren is able to pass training as well as Mikasa and Armin. Eren joins the survey corps because he wants to kill the titans. Mikasa and Armin follow Eren as well as many other trainees because they were motivated by Eren's speech. Soon after the graduation, the colossus titan breaks through the wall once more. The survey corps are sent to kill the titans and protect the citizens. Mikasa seemed to be the only newbie that could really kill any titans successfully.

    Armin is about to be eaten when Eren saves Armin and is eaten instead. When Mikasa was about to get killed, a mysterious titan saves her. The titan keeps fighting more titans, destroying them all. However, they all gang up on him and the titan is killed. When the titan starts to vanish, there seemed to be Eren who resided in its nape

    The other soilders want to kill Eren, saying he is a threat to society but Mikasa and Armin stand by Eren. The soilders try to fire a cannon at them, but Eren transforms into a titan to protect them from the blow. Eventually, Eren is to prove his worth by sealing up the hole in the wall that was broken by the collosall titan and was letting the titans in. When Eren transforms, he loses control and tries to kill Mikasa, but he snaps back to sense and carries a boulder to plug in the hole in the wall. This is when Levi makes his first appearance, when the survey corps return

    After, Eren has to undergo a trail to see under which groups' costody he will fall on. Levi beats up Eren to show the people that he is capable of handling Eren so they can leave Eren in his hands. And, after Eren's compassionate speech, the people agree to let Eren stay with Levi.
